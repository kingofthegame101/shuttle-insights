

# Shuttle Buddy Hub - Full Badminton Platform (Presentation Demo)

A comprehensive badminton platform demo with 6 core features, using mock data throughout for presentation purposes.

---

## 1. Landing Page & Navigation
- Hero section with badminton-themed branding ("Shuttle Buddy Hub")
- Clean navigation bar/sidebar with access to all 6 features
- Vibrant, sporty color scheme (greens, blues, energetic feel)
- Mobile-responsive layout

## 2. Book a Court
- Browse available badminton courts with filters (location, date, time, price)
- Court cards showing photos, ratings, amenities (parking, lighting, etc.)
- Booking flow: select court → pick date/time → confirm booking
- Mock confirmation with booking summary

## 3. Find a Group
- List of badminton groups/clubs looking for players
- Filter by skill level (beginner, intermediate, advanced), location, and schedule
- Group cards showing member count, play schedule, and skill level
- "Join Group" button with mock confirmation

## 4. Find Services
- Map-style or list view of nearby badminton services (string shops, equipment stores, physiotherapy)
- Category filters (equipment, stringing, sports medicine, etc.)
- Service cards with ratings, distance, contact info, and operating hours

## 5. Find a Coach
- Coach profiles with photo, bio, specialization, experience, and hourly rate
- Filter by skill focus (singles, doubles, footwork, smash technique)
- Rating/review display
- "Book a Session" mock flow

## 6. Video Analysis (NEW Feature ⭐)
- Upload interface for match/training videos
- Simulated AI processing with loading animation
- AI-generated feedback displayed in organized sections:
  - **Stroke Analysis** – technique ratings and improvement tips
  - **Footwork Assessment** – movement patterns and suggestions
  - **Tactical Analysis** – court positioning and shot selection
  - **Overall Score** with radar/spider chart visualization
- History of past analyses (mock data)

## 7. Info Hub
- Latest badminton news and BWF tournament updates (mock articles)
- Current competition schedules and results
- Player rankings display
- Links to BWF resources

---

**Design Approach:** Modern, sporty UI with card-based layouts, smooth transitions, and a consistent badminton-themed color palette. All data is mocked for demo/presentation purposes — no backend or authentication required.
